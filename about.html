About Me

Education and work experience

“

After graduating from Mahidol University Faculty of Information and Communication Technology in 2016, I debuted my career as a junior software engineer at QAD, an ERP solutions company. Then switched to a full-stack web developer at ReadyPlanet TravelTech, a leading company in digital marketing for hotels and resorts. Two years later, Jetabroad, an Australia-based competitive international airfares agency, offered me a software engineer position. However, with the effect of Coronavirus and travel restrictions, our team in Thailand is now dismissed. I joined the Ko-fi team and was developing a friendly platform for creators to earn money without a deducted fee from their supporters. I started a new chapter of my life again when I followed my dream of studying abroad and came to the University of Tartu to pursue a Master’s degree in Software Engineering.

”

Fields of interest
